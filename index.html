About Me

My name is Jerry Patrick Dickerson — personally I go by Patrick, but professionally I'm commonly referred to as Jerry. Born in Atlanta and raised in Stone Mountain, Georgia, I am 31 years old, with a birthday on May 18th. Growing up, baseball was my sport and my teacher; it shaped my discipline, my love of teamwork, and the person I've become. Spring is my favourite season — it stands for new beginnings, growth, and life. And one day, I plan to live near the beach, where I find the peace and clarity to do my best thinking.

I bring roughly 7 years of experience in finance across automotive, healthcare, investment banking, and private equity, along with a year spent as a programme instructor in education. My newest pursuit is software engineering — and I find the combination genuinely exciting. Finance tells you how to make decisions; engineering teaches you how to build things. I'm drawn to the space where those two disciplines meet, and my goal is to use that intersection to create meaningful projects that benefit my employer, myself, or the broader world.

At the highest level, my aspirations are straightforward: wealth, peace, and contribution. I care deeply about diplomacy, politics, and international affairs — the forces that shape life at a global scale. I aspire to pursue dual citizenship, travel widely, and immerse myself in different cultures and perspectives. Ultimately, I want to be involved in organisations and initiatives that make the world a meaningfully better place.
